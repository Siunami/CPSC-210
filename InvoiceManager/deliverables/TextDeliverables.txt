Task P3
To add a new service "Discount" which will not charge anything,
you first add enumeration "Discount" in ServiceType class.

Then you will have to add fields
DISC_CALLOUT
DISC_SERVICE_HOURLY
DISC_SERVICEPTS_BASE
DISC_SERVICEPTS_HOURLY
and assign them values you want.

Then you will have add implementation for enumeration "Discount" to
getServicePoints
getCalloutFee
getServiceFee
